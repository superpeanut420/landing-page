Best food to feed Guinea Pigs!

A classic! Pigs love to use hay as a resting spot, play area, but also as a quick snack. Having a supply of hay on hand is necessary to raising your own guinea pig!

Flipping sweet and epic! For pigs kiwi is a very safe, healthy, and tasty treat. They are ussually very cheap in the supermarket, and it is just so cute how they chomp on this fruit :D

Holy crap its the main character from Veggie Tales! Packed with a massive crunch, celery is a super healthy snack for guinea pigs. I ussually give mine a stalk everyday!

Unlock your pig's super vision! Carrots are perhaps the healthiest option out of all of these foods due to its high fiber which keeps your pet strong and healthy.
